# abbies® Boon Video Studio

**Generative music-reactive video creator — standalone HTML, no install, works offline.**

Drop your images. Drop your music. Watch it generate. Export your video.

🎬 **[Open the Studio](https://theabbiefree.github.io/abbies-boon-video-studio)**

---

## What It Does

Boon Video Studio takes your design images and music and generates a reactive video where your art moves and pulses to the beat. Bass drives scale, mids drive rotation, highs drive fade timing. Every song produces a different video from the same image pool.

Two creative modes:

- **Generative** — images spin, pulse, scale and fade in and out across the full 9:16 canvas, responding to the music in real time
- **Presentation** — images slide in one at a time at an angle, rotate to upright center-screen, hold with a gentle bass pulse, then fade out as the next arrives

Output is **9:16 format** — ready for Instagram Reels, TikTok, and YouTube Shorts.

## How To Use

### 1. Load Your Files
- **Design Images** — drop up to 100 PNG or JPG files. Your kaleidoscope designs, sacred geometry, characters, patterns — anything goes. Original colors, no colorization.
- **abbies® Logo** — drop your logo PNG. Appears bottom right, always visible.
- **Album Cover** — drop your album art. Appears bottom left, always visible.
- **Background** — choose a solid color, or drop an image and toggle between Stretch and Tile.
- **Music** — drop your MP3, WAV, or OGG file.

### 2. Choose Your Mode
Toggle between **Generative** and **Presentation** in the sidebar.

### 3. Dial In Your Feel (Generative mode)
Two live controls — adjust these **while the preview is playing**:
- **Spawn Rate** — how quickly new images appear. 1 is sparse and breathing, 10 is dense and busy.
- **Lifespan** — how long each image stays on screen before fading. 1 is fast and flickering, 10 is slow and lingering.

### 4. Preview
Hit **Preview** — the animation runs at low resolution with your music playing underneath. Watch the beat sync. Adjust the sliders live. Stop when you're happy with the settings.

### 5. Export
Hit **Export ZIP** — the studio renders every frame at full **1080×1920** and packages a ZIP containing:
- `abbies-boon-video-[timestamp].webm` — your video
- `music-[your-track-name]` — your music file
- `README.txt` — your export settings

### 6. Get Your MP4
Take the WebM and your music file to **[abbies® Boon Video Muxer](https://theabbiefree.github.io/abbies-boon-video-muxer)** — drop both files, hit Mux, download your finished MP4. Ready to post.

## Technical Notes

- **Format:** WebM (VP9) — converts to MP4 via the Boon Video Muxer
- **Resolution:** 1080×1920 (9:16)
- **Frame rate:** 24fps
- **No data leaves your machine** — all processing happens in your browser
- **Works offline** — once loaded, no internet required
- **Single HTML file** — open in any modern browser
